Who We Are
Read More
The Department of Palliative Medicine at Christian Medical College Vellore is dedicated to offering compassionate, whole-person care for patients with advanced cancers and chronic conditions like COPD, ILD, and renal failure. Our caring team of doctors, nurses, psychologists, social workers, and chaplains works closely with patients and their families to provide comfort, support, and hope through every step of their journey.

What We Do
Read More
Outpatient and Inpatient Care: We provide personalized care to improve quality of life through regular outpatient follow-ups and symptom management in our inpatient unit.
Home Care and Hospice Services: Our team offers home visits and compassionate end-of-life care at our 15-bed hospice, ensuring comfort when hospital or home care isn’t possible.
Counseling and Support:
We offer emotional, social, and spiritual support for patients and families through counseling, social care, and spiritual guidance.
Consultation and Bereavement: Our team provides consultative care in various wards and ongoing bereavement support for families after the loss of a loved one.
